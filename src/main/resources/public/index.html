About Us

Airsoft is a sport in which players battle each other through a variety of different game modes ranging from Team Death Match to Capture the flag. We here at Inferno CQB play fast paced and action packed Close Quarters Battle gametypes.

Inferno CQB is conveniently located in the heart of Tallaght just 5 mins walk from the Luas Red line. We are fully insured with ample parking, toilet facilities and CCTV in our gear up areas. Our friendly and good humoured staff have certified first aid training.

Proper fabricated buildings in conjunction with stairs, rooms, corridors, etc. Full proper lighting system, warm and comfortable reception area. Softdrinks and chocolate/crisps sold on site in our Cafe.

Full rental packages (airsoft gun, mask, overalls & unlimited ammo) available on site.

Gun Sales & Gun Tech

Looking to get your pride and joy repaired or upgraded? Why not drop into our resident Gun Technician who offers a wide range of upgrade services. Or drop him a message on his facebook page Devils Airsoft Supplies & Repairs

Maybe you're a little bored of your old gun, and want something new and exciting altogether, we also have a wide range of guns available to order from our in house store

Mac 10 Café

On site we have our kitted out Mac 10 Café, we have a fully operational coffee machine from Nespresso.
We also stock a range of soft drinks, energy drinks and bottled water.

You can also choose from our range of snacks and bars. A comfortable place to relax between your gaming experience and somewhere to unwind afterwards.
